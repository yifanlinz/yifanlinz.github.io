# Profile Photo

Place your profile photo here as `profile.jpg`.

## Photo Requirements

- **File name**: `profile.jpg`
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Size**: Recommended 300x300 pixels or larger (will be automatically resized)
- **Aspect ratio**: Square (1:1) works best for the circular crop
- **Quality**: High resolution for best results

## How to Add Your Photo

1. Save your photo as `profile.jpg` in this directory
2. The photo will automatically appear on your homepage
3. The image will be displayed as a circular profile photo with a subtle border and shadow

## Alternative Photo Names

If you want to use a different filename, update the `photo` setting in `_config.yml`:

```yaml
photo: "assets/images/your-photo-name.jpg"
```
